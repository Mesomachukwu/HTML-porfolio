Best K-drama According To Mimi

My Top 3 K-drama series

Extraordinary Attorney Woo

This series was just something different. Its about disability, particularly autism, and just a bit on romance.

Vicenzo

This is majorly about revenge and how it was executed. If you love revenge plot, i recommend 'The Glory'.

Reborn Rich

This is another revenge series. Its mainly about a guy that was reborn into the family that killed him. It has a bit of romance, well if you dont like romance I'll still recommend watching it because the romance is very little.
